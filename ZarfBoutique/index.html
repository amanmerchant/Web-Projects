Our Story

Our Journey

Bi'idhniAllahi ta'aala, Zarf was born from a shared struggle—finding true modest clothing that upholds dignity without compromise.

We seek to revive the Sunnah of the Sahabiyat by crafting abayas and khimars that reflect authentic Islamic modesty—affordable, elegant, and truly Shariah-compliant.

Zarf isn't just clothing—it's a mission, a revival, a commitment to faith.

Why Choose Zarf?

Faith-Driven Commitment

Zarf exists to serve the Deen, empowering sisters to uphold their Muslim identity without the pressure of trends.

Shariah-Compliant

Our garments are not just about fulfilling Shariah requirements—they represent grace, dignity, and faith.

Affordable Elegance

Because modesty should be effortless—accessible, high-quality attire designed with sincerity.
